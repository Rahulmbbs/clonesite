Medical Education

At Dr. Chandramma Dayananda Sagar Institute of Medical Education and Research (CDSIMER), we aim to encourage, educate and empower our students hailing from diverse backgrounds. Our focused approach involves patient-centered medical care through our understanding of latest developments in prevention and treatment of diseases. CDSIMER strives to develop individuals through the years in our campus with education, teaching and research in medical sciences so that they evolve into well-qualified medical professionals who are well equipped to holistically serve humanity, and aid the health and well-being of our community at large. Practicing at the hospital that is attached to the Institute will also help facilitate the medical students put into action learning from their classroom to actual patients in their senior years.

LIFE AT CDSIMER

CDSIMER benefits from a rich cultural heritage of DSU fortifying the lives of all its students during the years of graduate life spent in the University campus. The institution provides an invigorating atmosphere to all its stakeholders with an innate inspiration to enjoy the experience of learning.
